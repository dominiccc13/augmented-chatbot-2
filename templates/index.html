About This Chatbot

This RAG (retrieval-augmented generation) Chatbot was architected using FastAPI to serve asynchronous endpoints and an endpoint handling prompt engineering and prompt augmentation.

Vector embedding and cosine similarity is used to identify a similar prompt-response pair to augment OpenAI's GPT-5-Nano model with special prompt engineering.

This enables utilization of personalized data about me without fine tuning alongside the natural language generation of GPT-5-Nano technology.
